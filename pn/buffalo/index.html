About the Neighborhood
Buffalo Promise Neighborhood (BPN) is a 2011 grantee built on a unique public-private partnership comprised of 37 partners and providers. It brings together the leaders of local businesses, communities and philanthropies to help build and sustain stronger communities within the city of Buffalo. BPN serves three schools in the neighborhood: Children’s Academy, Highgate Heights Elementary, and Westminster Community Charter School.

Major strategies used
Periodic data-driven meetings between principles of target schools to review performance indicators such as student attendance rates
Partnership with AmeriCorps where volunteers provide teaching assistance, peer mentorship, and after school help
Implemented several changes to lunch room activities in target middle school based on recommendations by external consultant
Acquisition of 30 new lab computers for the elementary school library
Acquisition of “1:1” iPads for grades 3-6, and “1:1” HP laptops for grades 7 and 8
