About the Neighborhood
Led by California State University, East Bay, Hayward Promise Neighborhood (HPN) is a collaborative partnership working to ensure educational success and a safe, healthy, thriving community for all living in Hayward. The partnership works to provide comprehensive, high-quality educational and social support to students that attend six schools within the Hayward Unified School District, and to ensure the long-term health, safety and economic well-being of the entire community.

Major strategies used
Student success coaches provide case management to "at risk" students in high schools focusing on attendance and issues causing absenteeism
Youth Enrichment Program offers online credit recovery classes during summer break to high school students
Early Assessment Program to assess "college readiness" at the end of 11th grade and provide support to students who wish to attend college but who are not considered ready
Community oriented policing and code enforcement to foster neighborhood children
Neighborhood Partnership strategy to make the Neighborhood safer and less blighted
